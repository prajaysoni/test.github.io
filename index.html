Welcome to the Wonderful World of Powerlifting

This sport consists of three movements

  • The Squat
  • The Bench Press
  • The Deadlift

Basic Competition Rules

Each movement gets three/four attempts.

The highest and most successful attempt counts toward the competition total.

The competitor with the highest total is the winner for his/her weight class.
